About us

Our company is based in Pakistan, but our classes are conducted across the globe, with classes in the USA, the UK, Australia, Canada, Germany, Norway, Ireland, Switzerland, France, Italy, Iceland, Sweden, Denmark, Finland, Singapore, Hong Kong, Belgium, Spain, and the UAE. The company was formed by Tarteel Technologies with a mission to make high-quality learning of the Quran and Tajweed accessible to adults and children throughout the world. Our brand name Al-Saeed Online Quran Academy has become synonymous with that mission, and our graduates are spreading the word about what they have gained from studying Holy Quran with us..

Our Philosophy

We are driven to attain the blessings of Almighty Allah through bringing His word to Muslims throughout the world so that they can also seek this righteous path. We stress the importance of learning the science of Tajweed because the highest level of understanding comes when reciting the Quran with its rules for reading. We seek to bring the message of Almighty Allah, in its truest and most beautiful form, to people that are born Muslims, to converts, and to all that are interested in the Quranic teachings. Beyond the scripture, we seek to teach our students how to be role models as good Muslims, spreading light and understanding of the Quran to their communities.
